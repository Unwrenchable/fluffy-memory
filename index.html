🏛️ Social Security Disability Forms (SSDI/SSI)

Official forms needed for disability applications

📋 SSA-16-BK - Disability Application

Main SSDI application form. Start here if applying for disability benefits.

16 pages • Fillable PDF

📋 SSA-3368-BK - Adult Disability Report

Detailed information about your medical conditions, treatments, and limitations.

15 pages • Fillable PDF

⚠️ SSA-827 - Authorization to Release Information

CRITICAL: Fill one out for EACH doctor/hospital. SSA needs this to get your medical records!

Required • 2 pages • Fillable PDF

⚖️ SSA-561-U2 - Request for Reconsideration

For Appeals: Use this if your application was denied. File within 60 days!

Appeal Form • 4 pages • Urgent if Denied
